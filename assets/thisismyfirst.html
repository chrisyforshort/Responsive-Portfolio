Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon seasoned salt
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 chicken breasts, cut into small pieces
  • 2 cup seasoned breadcrumbs
  • Vegetable oil, for frying
  • Ketchup, for serving

Directions

  1. In a plastic bag, mix the flour, seasoned salt, salt, and pepper
  2. In a bowl, mix the eggs with 2 tablespoons of water
  3. In a separate bowl, add the breadcrumbs
  4. Shake the chicken pieces in the bag to coat
  5. In batches, dip them in the egg wash and then in the breadcrumbs
  6. Heat about 2 inches of vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat until a few breadcrumb dropped in the oil start to sizzle immediately
  7. Fry the chicken until golden brown and cooked through, a couple minutes per side
  8. Drain on a paper towel and serve with ketchup
